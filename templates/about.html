This is a website created by Paige Johnson, Andrew Demaree, and Brianna Daniels.

The intent of this app is to share photos of fish, enjoy Phish music, and be able to chat with fellow Phish-fish enthusiasts!


In this app, you can upload images via a dropdown menu once logged in.

Be careful as there is no way to delete a submitted image!

To view images, click the Go Phish button which shows after user creation!

Supported image types are:

  • JPG
  • JPEG
  • PNG
  • JFIF

The chat room can be accessed via the drop down menu as well!


You will need to authenticate your spotify account to take advantage of the music portion!
